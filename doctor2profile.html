ABOUT

Dr. Atul Garg is listed among the top cardiac surgeons in the country and has a vast surgical experience of over 20,000 cardiac surgeries. He is widely acknowledged for his expertise in treating Total Arterial Coronary Bypass Surgery, Paediatric Cardiac Surgery, Valve Repairs, Structural Heart Disease, and Surgery for Cardiac Failure. Dr. Garg is an expert in the management of heart failure, which includes - LVAD -Left Ventricular Assist Device (Artificial Heart); Heart Transplant, and Implantation of various devices for heart failure
